Linear Algebra

Linear algebra focuses on vector spaces and linear mappings between these spaces.

Key Concepts:

Matrix Operations Vector Spaces

Matrix Operations

Matrix operations form the computational foundation of linear algebra and its applications.

Explore Operations:

Matrix Multiplication Determinants

Matrix Multiplication

Matrix multiplication is a fundamental operation that combines two matrices to create a new matrix.

Key concepts include:

  • Row-column multiplication
  • Dimension compatibility
  • Applications in transformations

Determinants

Determinants help us understand the properties of linear transformations and solve systems of equations.

Important aspects include:

  • Calculating determinants
  • Properties of determinants
  • Applications in solving equations

Vector Spaces

Vector spaces provide a framework for studying linear algebra in an abstract setting.

  • Vector space axioms
  • Basis and dimension
  • Linear independence

Abstract Algebra

Abstract algebra studies algebraic structures such as groups, rings, and fields.

  • Group theory
  • Ring theory
  • Field extensions

Differential Calculus

Differential calculus focuses on rates of change and slopes of curves.

  • Derivatives
  • Rate of change
  • Optimization

Integral Calculus

Integral calculus deals with accumulation of quantities over time or space.

  • Definite integrals
  • Indefinite integrals
  • Applications in physics
